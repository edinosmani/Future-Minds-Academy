Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional soccer (football) player widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. He was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina. Messi is known for his incredible skill, agility, and goal-scoring prowess. He spent the majority of his professional career at FC Barcelona, where he became an iconic figure and won numerous domestic and international titles, including multiple UEFA Champions League and La Liga titles. During his time at Barcelona, Messi set numerous records and received numerous individual awards, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, which are given annually to the world's best player. In August 2021, Messi left Barcelona and joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) on a free transfer. He has also been a key player for the Argentine national team, helping them win the Copa America in 2021, which was a significant achievement for both Messi and his country.
